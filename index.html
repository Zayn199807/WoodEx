Welcome to Wood Exchange Fiji

Wood Exchange Fiji is a trusted platform connecting buyers and sellers of quality timber and wood products across Fiji. Through our active network and Facebook presence, we provide a professional space where contractors, builders, suppliers, and individuals can efficiently source and trade timber for residential and commercial projects.

Our mission is to strengthen Fiji’s timber and construction industry by promoting responsible forestry practices, reliable supply channels, and strong local partnerships. We are committed to supporting sustainable growth while ensuring customers have access to durable, climate-suitable materials.

At Wood Exchange Fiji, we stand for quality, transparency, and long-term value — helping build stronger structures and stronger business relationships throughout the country.
